### 1. Custom Domain (HIGHEST IMPACT) 🎯
**Why:** Single biggest factor for SEO ranking
**Cost:** ~₹700-1200/year
**Where:** Namecheap, GoDaddy, or Hostinger

Steps:
1. Buy domain (sathishkumar.dev or sathishm.dev)
2. Go to Vercel dashboard → Settings → Domains
3. Add your custom domain
4. Update DNS records (Vercel will guide you)
5. Wait 24-48 hours for propagation
6. Come back and we'll update your metadata in one go

**Impact:** 📈 Massive boost to search rankings

### 2. Google Search Console Setup 🔍
**Why:** See keywords people use to find you
**Time:** 5 minutes
**Cost:** Free

Steps:
1. Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add property"
3. Enter your custom domain (once live)
4. Verify ownership (DNS or HTML file)
5. Submit sitemap (we'll generate this)
6. Monitor keywords & rankings

**What you'll see:**
- Keywords people search for
- Your average ranking position
- Click-through rate from search results
- Search impressions
