## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Be patient**: SEO takes 3-6 months to show results
2. **Focus on quality**: Write good content, not keyword-stuffed content
3. **Monitor regularly**: Check Search Console weekly
4. **Build backlinks**: Quality links matter more than quantity
5. **Update content**: Keep your portfolio fresh and updated
6. **Track everything**: Use spreadsheets to track progress
7. **Learn continuously**: Stay updated with SEO best practices

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Don't change title/description too frequently
- ❌ Don't keyword stuff (looks spammy)
- ❌ Don't ignore Search Console errors
- ❌ Don't forget to update sitemap when adding content
- ❌ Don't expect results in 1-2 weeks
- ❌ Don't buy backlinks (violates Google policies)
- ❌ Don't hide text or use cloaking
- ❌ Don't duplicate content across pages
